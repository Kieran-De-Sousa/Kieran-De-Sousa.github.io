Brains N' Brawn

Brains N` Brawn is a 3D beat 'em up puzzle game created in Unity Engine version 2020.1. A five-day game jam conducted at my university, the games scope was continuously adjusted and tweaked as each day passed due to the large disparity in skill sets and skill levels of our five-member team. The process was very rewarding and being my first game jam in person it was exciting planning and documenting with the team face to face.

Contributions:

  • Health Scripting
  • Melee Enemy AI (Navigation, Attacking)
  • Interactable Elements
